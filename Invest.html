Although it costs money to do

Investing is a great way to make revenue without work

This segment will be quite short because the concept is simple. Download an investing app like Robinhood, Nerdwallet, or Webull. Then check the stock market to see who is succeeding. Those are the shares you want to buy. Companies such as Apple, and Amazon are usually gaining shares, so invest $200-$500 into one if not both of them. You could either make back less than you gave, or a whole bunch more, depending on how much money the company is recieving.



Some would say this is just a different, safer version of gambling, because even if the stock is going down, you recieve something back. That is the silver lining in stock investing.
